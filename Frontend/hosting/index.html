Welcome to Space Snap

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to travel to locations in our solar system and collect samples for testing. You will be given a list of items that are needed, but these items have to be a perfect match with what you find. To make sure you are collecting the best samples, we want you to send us a picture of what you are collecting so we can analyze it and determine if it will pass or not. Make sure you look all over for the best potential sample you can find. Once your item passes, you will be granted permission to proceed to the next location. Good luck!
